Youshen Xiao

I am Youshen Xiao, a second-year M.S student in HISLab at ShanghaiTech University (Shanghai, China), advised by Prof. Fei Gao. Now, I have also joined SMILELab, advised by Prof.Yuyao Zhang. Before that, I received my B.E. degree at University of Shanghai for Science and Technology, Shanghai in 2023.

My research interests mainly focus on:

  • Photoacoustic Imaging Spare-View Reconstruction
  • Undersampling Photoacoustic Microscopy Reconstruction
  • Quantitative PAT
  • Neural Representation

See more in the detailed CV.

Feel free to find me in any way you like :)
